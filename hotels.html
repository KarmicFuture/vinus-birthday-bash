Can't crash at the villa? Stay five minutes away.

First choice: the Airbnb

Covered
Beds at the villa are first come, first served — RSVP to claim one. If the house fills up, or you'd just rather have your own room and roll in for the festivities, these nearby spots keep you close to home base. All are within about 15 minutes of the villa and Epcot.
The Splurge
Margaritaville Resort Orlando
~5 min to villa · ~15 min to Epcot · est. $250–$350/night
Practically next door. Stylish cottages and rooms, lagoon-style pools, and its own restaurant row at Sunset Walk. The easiest "walk of fame" back after nightcaps.
The Splurge
Gaylord Palms Resort
~12 min to villa · ~15 min to Epcot · est. $280–$400/night
A giant glass atrium with indoor gardens, a full spa, and ICE! holiday shows in December. A destination in itself if you want resort-level pampering.
Closest to Epcot
Walt Disney World Swan & Dolphin
~20 min to villa · walk/boat to Epcot · est. $300–$420/night
Inside the Disney bubble — you can walk or take a Friendship boat straight into Epcot's back entrance for Saturday. Ideal if Epcot is your main event.
The Sweet Spot
Embassy Suites Lake Buena Vista South
~10 min to villa · ~12 min to Epcot · est. $160–$220/night
Two-room suites, free cooked breakfast, and a nightly reception with complimentary drinks. Great for families who need space without villa chaos.
The Sweet Spot
Delta Hotels Orlando Celebration
~10 min to villa · ~15 min to Epcot · est. $140–$190/night
Recently renovated Marriott property next to charming downtown Celebration. Solid rooms, good pool, easy I-4 access for the Saturday carpool.
The Smart Buy
Holiday Inn Resort Orlando Suites — Waterpark
~8 min to villa · ~15 min to Epcot · est. $120–$180/night
Kid heaven: an on-site waterpark, themed kids' suites, and casual dining. The budget-friendly pick that the little ones will brag about.

Booking Tips

Book Early
Early December is peak festival season at Disney — Festival of Holidays crowds and youth sports weekends fill rooms fast. Lock in a refundable rate now.
Compare Rates
Check the hotel's own site against the usual booking sites, and use AAA, points, or member rates. Prices above are estimates — they swing with demand.
Stay in the Loop
Wherever you sleep, everything kicks off at the villa. Group dinners, pool time, and the Saturday carpool all leave from Windsor at Westside.
