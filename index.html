02
Around the World
Saturday · December 5, 2026
Epcot Day
  • 8:00 AM
    Coffee & Mickey Waffles at the House
    The Rat Pack does not skip breakfast. Fuel up before the main event. Kids eat, adults caffeinate.
  • 9:30 AM
    Depart for Epcot
    ~15 min drive. Park opens at 9 AM — arriving by 9:30 still lets you beat mid-morning crowds. Parking at EPCOT: $35/car.
  • 10 AM – 2 PM
    Future World / Main Park Attractions
    Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind (book Lightning Lane in advance), Remy's Ratatouille Adventure, Spaceship Earth, Test Track. Knock out the rides while energy is high.
  • 2 PM – Close
    World Showcase — Drinks Around the World
    The main event. Travel the World Showcase clockwise, stopping at each pavilion for a signature drink and food bite.
    Mexico — Margarita Norway — Aquavit China — Tsing Tao Germany — Schnapps or Beer Italy — Limoncello America — Bourbon Japan — Sake France — Wine Morocco — Mint Tea UK — Pint Canada — Caesar
  • 9 PM
    Luminous — The Symphony of Us
    Epcot's nightly spectacular on World Showcase Lagoon. End the Epcot day on a high note with the whole crew.
  • ~10 PM
    Back to the House
    Hydrate, feet up, relive the evening. The quiet heroes keep the party going for those with any remaining energy.
December also means the Epcot International Festival of the Holidays — running Thanksgiving through Dec 30. Each country pavilion adds special holiday food and entertainment. Exceptional timing.

Rat Pack Tips

Dress Code
Lean into the theme. Fedoras, blazers, mouse ears with a gold band. Even the kids get ears. One group outfit photo is mandatory.
Epcot Logistics
Book Lightning Lane for Guardians of the Galaxy early — it sells out. The World Showcase doesn't open until 11 AM, so hit rides first.
Kids at Epcot
Remy's Ratatouille Adventure, Frozen Ever After, Finding Nemo, and Turtle Talk with Crush are all hits. Get kids a passport to stamp at each country.
Festival of Holidays
Running Dec 4–30 — perfect timing. Each country adds special holiday treats and performances.
Grocery Run
Publix, Walmart, and Target are all within 5 min of Windsor at Westside. Stock up Friday afternoon before drinks.
Transportation
Carpool or rideshare to Epcot Saturday. From Tampa, it's about 1 hr via I-4.
